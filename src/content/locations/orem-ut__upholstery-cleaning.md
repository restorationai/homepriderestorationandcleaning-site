## Why Orem Furniture Gets Dirtier Faster

## Our Upholstery Cleaning Process in Orem

Every job starts with a fiber identification check before a drop of moisture touches the piece. For fabric upholstery, we use a low-moisture hot-water extraction method calibrated to Utah Valley's hard water. That means pre-treating with a conditioner that neutralizes mineral ions before the cleaning solution goes down, which prevents the chalky residue that plagues DIY attempts. Leather upholstery cleaning follows a separate protocol: pH-balanced leather cleaner, light agitation with a soft-bristle brush, and a conditioner applied while the leather is still slightly damp so it absorbs rather than sits on the surface.
