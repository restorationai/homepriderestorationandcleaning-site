## Our Odor Removal and Deodorization Process in Orem

Every job starts with an odor mapping walkthrough — we identify the source, the migration path, and the materials affected before any equipment is staged. That step matters more than most homeowners expect, because treating the symptom (the smell in the living room) without addressing the source (smoke residue inside the HVAC plenum, or urine-saturated OSB under tile) produces results that last weeks, not years.

Depending on what we find, we deploy one or more of the following methods:

- **Thermal fogging** — a solvent-based fog that follows the same pathways smoke traveled, neutralizing odor molecules embedded in wall cavities, insulation, and ceiling voids.
- **Hydroxyl deodorization** — UV-generated hydroxyl radicals that break down odor compounds at the molecular level. Safe to run in occupied spaces, which matters when a family can't leave their home for days.
- **Ozone treatment** — high-concentration ozone for unoccupied spaces with severe smoke or biological odors. Requires full evacuation of people and pets, and we coordinate timing carefully.
- **HEPA air scrubbing and duct cleaning** — essential in homes where forced-air systems have circulated contaminated air, which is common in Orem's newer open-plan construction.
