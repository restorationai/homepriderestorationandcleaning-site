## Our Burst Pipe Cleanup and Repair Process in South Jordan

The first thing our technicians do on arrival is locate and confirm the water has been shut off — either at the main shutoff inside the home or at the curb stop. If the homeowner hasn't been able to reach the curb stop, we carry a curb key and can handle it. From there, the process moves through four concrete phases:

**1. Rapid extraction.** Truck-mounted extractors pull standing water from hard floors and carpet within the first hour. We pay particular attention to subfloor cavities, which are common in South Jordan's two-story homes where a pipe burst on the upper level saturates the structural assembly between floors.

**2. Moisture mapping.** Thermal imaging cameras and calibrated moisture meters map exactly how far water has wicked into walls, insulation, and framing. This step drives every drying decision — we don't guess, and we don't pull drywall unnecessarily.

**3. Structural drying.** Industrial low-grain refrigerant dehumidifiers and high-velocity air movers run continuously, typically for three to five days depending on material saturation. We monitor daily and adjust equipment placement as readings drop.
