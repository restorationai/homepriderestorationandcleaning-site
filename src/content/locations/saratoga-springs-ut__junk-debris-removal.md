## Local Note: Hard Water and Appliance Disposal

Saratoga Springs sits in one of Utah's hardest-water zones, and that affects appliances more than most homeowners realize. Water heaters, washing machines, and dishwashers here tend to fail earlier than their rated lifespan because of mineral buildup, which means appliance removal is a more frequent part of local junk jobs than in softer-water markets. Old water heaters in particular can be extremely heavy when sediment has calcified at the bottom — a standard 40-gallon tank can weigh 150 pounds or more once that scale accumulates.
