## Our Sewage Cleanup and Sanitization Process in Heber City

When we arrive on-site, the first priority is stopping the source and assessing how far contaminated water has traveled. Raw sewage migrates fast — it wicks into subfloor sheathing, insulation, and wall cavities within minutes of contact. Extraction comes next. We use truck-mounted and portable extraction units to pull standing waste water, followed by the removal of any porous materials — carpet, pad, drywall, insulation — that absorbed Category 3 contamination.
