## Our Carpet Cleaning Process in Eagle Mountain

Every job starts with a walkthrough to identify high-traffic lanes, stain types, and any areas where pet dander or allergens have concentrated — common in the larger floor-plan homes built throughout the Ranches, where open-concept layouts mean a single great room can carry a lot of square footage of carpet in one continuous run.

From there, the process moves through four concrete steps:

2. **Hot-water extraction** — Truck-mounted equipment operating at 200°F+ water temperature and high vacuum pressure pulls suspended soil and cleaning solution out of the fiber. The truck-mount stays outside, so there's no equipment noise or fumes inside your home.

4. **Carpet grooming and speed-dry** — Pile is raked in a uniform direction to speed drying and restore texture. In Eagle Mountain's dry climate, most rooms are dry within two to four hours under normal ventilation.
