From a burst pipe at 2 a.m. to a kitchen fire that leaves soot on every surface, property damage rarely waits for business hours. Home Pride Restoration and Cleaning handles the full arc of recovery — emergency mitigation, structural drying, debris removal, and complete reconstruction — for homeowners, landlords, property managers, and commercial clients across Saratoga Springs and the surrounding Wasatch Front communities. One company, one point of contact, start to finish.

## Emergency restoration services

When water is still moving, smoke is still settling, or a hazard is still present, the first hours determine how much of the structure and contents can be saved. Home Pride's IICRC-certified technicians respond around the clock to **water damage** — burst pipes, appliance failures, sewer backups, and flooding — extracting standing water and placing industrial drying equipment before secondary damage sets in. **Fire and smoke damage** response goes beyond visible char: smoke residue penetrates drywall cavities, HVAC systems, and soft goods, and it keeps reacting with surfaces long after the flames are out. **Storm damage** — hail, wind, and the freeze-thaw cycles that are routine along the Wasatch Front every winter — often combines structural breach with water intrusion, requiring rapid tarping and board-up alongside water mitigation. **Biohazard and trauma cleanup** rounds out the emergency side; that work follows strict OSHA protocols and proper disposal procedures to protect everyone on-site and in the building afterward.

## Reconstruction and renovation

Mitigation stops the damage. Reconstruction brings the property back. Once drying and remediation are complete, Home Pride's rebuild crews handle drywall, framing, flooring, cabinetry, painting, and finish carpentry — the same scope a general contractor would manage, coordinated directly with the mitigation documentation your insurance adjuster already has. Because the same company that dried the structure is rebuilding it, there are no gaps in the scope of work, no finger-pointing between trades, and no restart delays while a second contractor gets up to speed. This matters especially on larger losses where a Utah winter or a tight rental vacancy window makes schedule predictability critical.

## Specialty services

Some damage is slower and quieter. **Mold inspection and remediation** addresses the colonies that establish within 24–72 hours of undetected moisture — behind baseboards, inside wall cavities, under subfloor — and the remediation process follows established containment and removal protocols. **Odor removal** uses hydroxyl generators and thermal fogging to neutralize smoke, pet, and biological odors at the molecular level rather than masking them. **Board-up and tarping** services secure a property immediately after fire, storm, or vandalism when the structure is open to weather or unauthorized entry.

The practical advantage of working with one company across all of these phases is straightforward: the technician who documents the moisture readings on day one is connected to the project manager scheduling drywall on day fourteen.
